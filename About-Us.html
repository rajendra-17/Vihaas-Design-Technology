VihaasDT Welcomes You!!

We are a pioneer in the field of telecom for Homeland Security and Military applications. Our promoters / advisors have highest possible technical degrees and are specialised in their respective fields, having more than fifteen years of Industry/Project experience with project management and have delivered concurrent multiple projects with quality and complete customer satisfaction.

We have conducted comprehensive studies and researches on how to integrate coalition forces interactions effectively into a cohesive, flexible, and secure communication network, thereby ensuring a cooperative amalgamation of distinct forces into one.

Communication solutions are composed of discrete systems that caters to various communication needs of users in all military platforms. Communication Systems are the first range of products developed indigenously at VIHAAS. The experience, that has been accumulated over the years, is integrated with the latest technological developments in order to offer seamless, secure, fast and cost-effective communication solutions.

The communication systems are customized and designed exclusively to fulfil demands of Land, Airborne and Naval platforms for a secure and seamless voice, data and video communication.

Corporate Social Responsibility

At Vihaas Design Technologies,we believe that caring for the community around us is a way forward in developing a deeper appreciation of people and life. Social responsibility has always been a priority for the Vihaas Design Technologies family, and they have conducted business with the deep-seated purpose of making a positive difference to the society at large.

We have developed a thoughtful and inclusive approach, which is aligned with our values and involve participation of members across all functions. We focus our community engagement and programs on specific societal issues in three core areas -health, education and livelihood.
